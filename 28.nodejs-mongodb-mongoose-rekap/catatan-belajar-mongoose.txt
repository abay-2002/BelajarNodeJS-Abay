=== Things we can do with mongoose ===

With mongoose we could write a simpler syntax of mongodb 
writing MongoDB validation, casting and business logic boilerplate is a drag. That's why we wrote Mongoose.

Three main concept of mongoose/mongoose basics
1. Schema	Schema is how your data is structured.
2. Model	Model is the form of that structured data.
3. query	query is how you play the data against the database.

Features of mongoose
*Schema types
*Schema validation
*Schema middleware
*Schema method/vituals
*query
